Transforming note to blog posts

Writing blog is a great way to force myself to polish the writings, improving my notes in term of writing, clarity and idea. I keep a lots of notes with many different ideas and thoughts, but they are not well maintained. As a result, they are not very useful for myself or for others. Turning these notes into blog posts are a great way.

However, the blog posts I am working on is somewhat detached from my own org system. It’s this system that contains the knowledge. It’s only natural to have the blog posts to be another view of the knowledge base, or the main system. Put it this way, the blog posts are basically outlet of notes that I am happy about. Period. Nothing more and certainly nothing less.

The solution is a way to publish new blog posts directly from the org notes. Put it in another way, I need to publish my org notes.

ox-hugo is great. I can use it to generate new blog posts in markdown. Further more, writing stuffs in my org-wiki is much better for retrieving information later.
